Dress Code

We do not have a strict dress code. Guests are invited to wear their Saturday best to the church. There is no strict requirement for women or men to wear a tie, or for either to wear hats. Any colour (color) is acceptable. Anyone who has attended the famous Trinity Ball will also remember that Front Square is not great for those wearing heels, but recent improvements mean that there is a solid perpendicular path that avoids the cobblestones.
