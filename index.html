Church Ceremony

A Catholic ceremony will take place at the Trinity College Chapel at 2pm. Please take your seats inside the church (either side) by 13:50.

Reception and Dinner

After the ceremony, we will celebrate at the Fitzpatrick Castle Hotel in Killiney, about 15km south-east of Trinity. A drinks reception will be held from 15:30-17:30 in the Dungeon Bar, after which we will have dinner in the Albert & Behan Suite. After dinner there will be music from a band followed by a disc jockey. Later on in the evening there will be more hot food. If your thirst is not quenched by the time the bar closes at half two, the resident's bar will be open until four.

Morning Swim

If anyone is staying in Killiney, the next morning we will shaking off the hangover at the nearby Vico, the finest bathing place in Dublin.
