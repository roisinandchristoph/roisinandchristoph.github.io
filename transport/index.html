Logistics

To Trinity

If driving, Trinity does not have its own parking facilities, but there are many parking garages very close to the college. For example (all similarly-priced per hour):
  • Brown Thomas, 49 Clarendon St.
  • Q-Park Setanta Place, Molesworth Street
  • Park Rite, 52-57 Fleet Street
If taking public transportation, Trinity College is very close to the Trinity Luas Stop, Tara or Pearse Street DART/commuter rail stations. Dozens of bus routes also pass through College Green (check Google Maps for bus routes from your hotel).

To Fitzpatrick Castle Hotel

If driving, The Fitzpatrick Castle Hotel has on-site parking which is free of charge, and there is also street parking nearby.
We have arranged for a 50-seat minibus to the Fitzpatrick Castle Hotel from Trinity after the ceremony. It will depart from Nassau Street at 15:00.
If you miss this, the DART commuter train from either Tara St. or Pearse St. to Dalkey station is another alternative.
The bar at the reception will run very late so guests can leave by taxi at their own leisure.
