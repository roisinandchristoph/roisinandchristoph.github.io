Transport

To Trinity

Trinity does not have its own parking facilities, but there are many parking garages very close to the college. For example (all similarly-priced per hour):
  • Brown Thomas, 49 Clarendon St.
  • Q-Park Setanta Place, Molesworth Street
  • Park Rite, 52-57 Fleet Street
If taking public transportation, Trinity College very well connected to the Luas, DART and bus lines:
  • Luas (tram): Trinity Stop
  • DART (train): Pearse Street
  • Bus: Many bus routes pass through College Green or Nassau Street, both right next to Trinity. Check Google Maps for bus routes.

    To Fitzpatrick Castle Hotel from Trinity

    If driving, The Fitzpatrick Castle Hotel has on-site parking which is free of charge, and there is also street parking nearby.
